Why Choose the Bed 'n Biscuit for Pet Boarding?

Unlike standalone boarding facilities, the Bed 'n Biscuit is located within Flint River Animal Hospital. This means your pet benefits from on-site veterinary supervision throughout their stay. Our dedicated boarding staff provides:

  • Clean, climate-controlled accommodations
  • Daily exercise and outdoor time
  • Feeding according to your pet's regular diet and schedule
  • Medication administration if needed
  • 24/7 on-site staff supervision
  • Immediate veterinary care if health concerns arise

Boarding Hours

Drop-off and pick-up: Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Sunday boarding pick-up is available from 3:30 PM – 4:30 PM only. Saturday pick-up is not available.

To reserve boarding, call us at (256) 852-3300 or contact us online.

Paws 2 Play Doggie Daycare in Huntsville

Need a place for your dog to socialize, exercise, and burn off energy during the day? Paws 2 Play Doggie Daycare at Flint River Animal Hospital offers a safe, supervised environment where your dog can play and interact with other dogs throughout the day.

  • Supervised group play sessions with size-appropriate groups
  • Indoor and outdoor play areas
  • Rest periods throughout the day
  • Socialization with other friendly dogs
  • Daily report on your dog's activities
  • Great alternative to leaving your dog home alone

Doggie daycare is available Monday through Friday during regular business hours. Contact us for availability and enrollment.
