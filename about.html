Our Story

Independently Owned, Community Focused

Flint River Animal Hospital was constructed and opened for services on April 2, 2009, by founder Dr. Kevin Lowe. After graduating from Auburn University College of Veterinary Medicine in 2002, Dr. Lowe gained experience at veterinary hospitals in Chattanooga, Washington D.C., and Atlanta before returning home to Huntsville to build his own practice.

Today, Flint River Animal Hospital is a state-of-the-art, digitally integrated, full-service veterinary hospital with a primary focus on client education. We are proudly independent — privately owned and operated, not affiliated with any corporation.

Our commitment to excellence has been recognized nationally. FRAH was selected by the Huntsville/Madison County Chamber of Commerce as the 2012 Small Business of the Year for Professional Services and by the U.S. Chamber of Commerce as one of the Top 100 Small Businesses of the Year (2013 Blue Ribbon Award).

Our Veterinary Team

Meet the Doctors at Flint River Animal Hospital

Our team of five experienced veterinarians brings diverse expertise and a shared passion for exceptional animal care.

👨‍⚕️

Dr. Kevin Lowe, DVM

Owner & Founder

A Huntsville native and Auburn University graduate (DVM, 2002). Dr. Lowe was a stunt man on the Auburn cheerleading team before trading his athletic scholarship for a position in Auburn's Veterinary Parasitology Lab. He brings extensive experience from practices across the Southeast to lead FRAH.

👩‍⚕️

Dr. Carr, DVM

Veterinarian

Dr. Carr earned her Bachelor's in Animal Science from Rutgers University and her DVM from the University of Missouri in 2006. She moved to Huntsville in late 2020 and has been a valued member of the FRAH team since December 2020.

👩‍⚕️

Dr. Struthers, DVM

Veterinarian

A Huntsville High School graduate, Dr. Struthers completed her undergraduate studies at Mississippi State and earned her DVM from Auburn University in 2016. She previously worked at FRAH as a vet technician and surgery assistant before joining as a full-time veterinarian.

👩‍⚕️

Dr. Laura Manasco, DVM

Veterinarian

Dr. Manasco received her Bachelor of Science, Master's of Agriculture, and DVM all from Auburn University. She has practiced as a rural vet and in small animal practices, developing a special interest in soft tissue surgery and reproduction.

👩‍⚕️

Dr. Rice, DVM

Veterinarian

Originally from Northport, Alabama, Dr. Rice earned her Bachelor's in Biology from the University of Alabama in Tuscaloosa and her DVM from Auburn University College of Veterinary Medicine in 2017.
