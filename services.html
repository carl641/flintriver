What We Offer

Full-Service Veterinary Hospital

Our medical team at Flint River Animal Hospital is prepared for everything from regular wellness exams to same-day emergency care. All services are provided in our state-of-the-art, digitally integrated facility.

Pet Wellness Exams & Preventive Care

Regular wellness exams are the foundation of your pet's long-term health. At Flint River Animal Hospital, our veterinarians perform thorough physical examinations, assess your pet's overall health, and develop customized prevention plans tailored to your pet's age, breed, and lifestyle.

  • Comprehensive annual and semi-annual physical exams
  • Puppy and kitten wellness programs
  • Senior pet health screenings
  • Nutritional counseling and weight management
  • Parasite prevention (heartworm, flea, tick)
  • Health certificates and travel documentation

Our client education focus means we take the time to explain findings, answer questions, and help you make informed decisions for your pet's health.

Pet Vaccinations & Immunizations

Vaccinations are essential for protecting your pet from dangerous and potentially life-threatening diseases. Our veterinarians develop individualized vaccination schedules based on your pet's risk factors, lifestyle, and age.

  • Core vaccinations for dogs (rabies, distemper, parvovirus, adenovirus)
  • Core vaccinations for cats (rabies, FVRCP)
  • Lifestyle-based non-core vaccines (Bordetella, Leptospirosis, Lyme, FeLV)
  • Puppy and kitten vaccination series
  • Annual booster vaccinations
  • Vaccination records and documentation

Pet Surgery & Dental Care

Our surgical team performs a wide range of procedures in our modern, fully equipped surgical suite. From routine spay and neuter procedures to complex soft tissue surgeries, your pet receives the highest standard of surgical care with comprehensive pain management.

Surgical Services

  • Spay and neuter (ovariohysterectomy, castration)
  • Soft tissue surgery
  • Mass and tumor removal
  • Abdominal surgery
  • Wound repair and reconstruction
  • Pre-surgical bloodwork and anesthesia monitoring

Dental Care

Dental disease affects over 80% of pets by age three. Our dental services include:

  • Professional dental cleanings under anesthesia
  • Dental radiographs (X-rays)
  • Tooth extractions
  • Oral health assessments
  • At-home dental care recommendations

In-House Diagnostics & Laboratory

Fast, accurate diagnoses lead to better treatment outcomes. Our in-house diagnostic laboratory enables us to run tests and receive results quickly — often within the same visit — so treatment can begin promptly.

  • Complete blood count (CBC) and blood chemistry panels
  • Urinalysis and fecal testing
  • Digital radiography (X-rays)
  • Heartworm and tick-borne disease testing
  • FeLV/FIV testing for cats
  • Cytology and skin scrapings
  • Pre-anesthetic testing

Emergency & Urgent Veterinary Care

When your pet needs immediate medical attention, Flint River Animal Hospital is here to help. We offer same-day emergency appointments during business hours and after-hours emergency services to ensure your pet receives timely care.

  • Same-day emergency appointments (Monday–Friday, 7 AM – 6 PM)
  • After-hours emergency services
  • Trauma and acute illness treatment
  • Emergency surgery capabilities
  • Stabilization and critical care

If your pet is experiencing an emergency, call us immediately at (256) 852-3300.

Pet Microchipping

Microchipping is a safe, permanent form of identification that significantly increases the chances of reuniting with your pet if they become lost. The procedure is quick, minimally invasive, and can be performed during a regular office visit.

  • Quick, minimally invasive implantation
  • Permanent, tamper-proof identification
  • National pet recovery database registration
  • Can be done during any office visit or surgical procedure

In-House Pet Pharmacy

Our on-site pharmacy carries a comprehensive selection of veterinary medications, preventatives, and therapeutic diets. This means your pet can begin treatment immediately without an extra trip to an outside pharmacy.

  • Prescription medications
  • Heartworm, flea, and tick preventatives
  • Therapeutic and prescription diets
  • Supplements and nutraceuticals

Professional Pet Grooming in Huntsville

Keep your pet looking and feeling great with our professional grooming services. Our experienced groomers work with dogs and cats of all breeds and sizes, providing personalized grooming tailored to your pet's needs.

  • Full baths and blow-drying
  • Breed-specific haircuts and styling
  • Nail trimming and grinding
  • Ear cleaning
  • De-shedding treatments
  • Medicated and specialty shampoo baths

Grooming is great for more than just looks — it helps prevent skin issues, matting, ear infections, and overgrown nails. Contact us to schedule a grooming appointment.
